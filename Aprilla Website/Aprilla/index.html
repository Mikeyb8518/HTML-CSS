About The RS660

The 660-cc twin-cylinder 100-horsepower engine offers exhilarating acceleration
and impressive torque, much of it even at low revs. An exceptionally harmonious
engine delivers the performance of a much larger twin-cylinder, but it is so compact
and lightweight that Aprilia's engineers can encase it in an agile, hardworking
chassis that is suitable for both the road and the track.

The RS 660 high-quality riding experience owes much to technological features such
as the colour TFT display with special graphics and APRC settings for complete control
over riding parameters at all times. The ability to choose between 2 predefined and 3
customisable riding modes depending on the track or road conditions takes the enjoyment
factor up another level.
